Altrone
In 2019 I joined Apple Foundation Program Batch 1. In that program I and my teammate developped a prototype app for gamers. It's name was Altrone. Altrone was an app to find game based on your play time. It also help you to set frequency and duration to get the best game recommendation which fit your play time. This app can be useful for gamer who had a limited time for playing.
Tebak Skuy
In the final assignment of Algorithm & Programming in the first semester at Informatics@UC, I and my patrner, Reiner Anggriawan Jasin, create a simple prototype program called as TebakSkuy. TebakSkuy was a game about funny riddle that also test your logic. The goal from this game was to bring you more joy and knowledge about funny riddle. This game was showed in My First App Exhibition at Universitas Ciputra.
Website UKM Jepang
In 2019 I and my friends were contribute in making a website for UKM Task Force Sakura at Universitas Ciputra. We realised UKM Task Force Sakura need more media to publish their activity at society. Beside, they also need a media that can easily promote their activities and events to all students at Universitas Ciputra, so that many students will have interest in joining them. That thing made me and my friends at informatics create a website for them, so UKM Task Force Sakura can be more improved. There are many interesting features in this website you can check them all by clicking this link : bit.ly/TaskForceSakura.
