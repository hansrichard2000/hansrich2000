Altrone
In 2019 I joined Apple Foundation Program Batch 1. In that program I and my teammate developped a prototype app for gamers. It's name was Altrone. Altrone was an app to find game based on your play time. It also help you to set frequency and duration to get the best game recommendation which fit your play time. This app can be useful for gamer who had a limited time for playing.

Students Assistant App
In 2020 I and my friends were contribute in making an app during the pandemic COVID-19. This app called Students Assistant. Do you always forget your task or homework? Do you want to make your own notes for lesson with easily? Student Assistant can be your solution.
